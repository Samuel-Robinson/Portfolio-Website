Hey there! Thanks for taking a moment to look through my portfolio. My name is Sam. Sam I am. I'm a User Experience Designer who has just started out my incredible journey in the field of UX. I am currently a second year student at Wilfrid Laurier University, where I am majoring in UX. Among other things, I have already participated in a large variety of related courses, performed group work, and presented our solutions.

I love all aspects of design. From conducting interviews and user research, to synthesizing information; from prototyping and testing, to UI design; I love it all! It is my goal to advocate for the needs of normal people - and I think that shows in my work.

When I'm not solving people's problems, I like to spend my time working on side projects (like coding this website), travelling, and studying my Bible. I am a dedicated Bible student and believer in God, and I hope that shows in the way I conduct myself in everyday life. If you have any questions, feel free to contact me and I'd be happy to chat!

I'm incredibly excited for where this field might take me next. Whatever comes my way, I'll be ready to take it on. Here's to the future!
